ABOUT US

Surabaya Ink Well is where bold ideas meet skilled hands. Located in the heart of Surabaya since 2022, our studio is dedicated to creating tattoos that are raw, personal, and unforgettable. Every piece we craft is designed with precision, passion, and respect for the art of tattooing.

From minimalist symbols to full custom pieces, we turn your vision into ink that lasts a lifetime. Ready to wear your story? Book your session at Surabaya Ink Well and let our artists bring your next tattoo to life.
